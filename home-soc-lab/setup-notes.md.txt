## Troubleshooting Notes

During the Windows 11 VM setup, I ran into repeated black-screen issues after installation. I troubleshot by checking VirtualBox storage, removing the ISO after install, confirming the virtual hard disk was present, adjusting display settings, and restarting the VM until Windows successfully reached the login screen.

This helped me practice basic virtualization troubleshooting, boot troubleshooting, and careful step-by-step problem solving.
